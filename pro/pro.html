IELTS stands for the International English Language Testing System. It is a test of English language proficiency developed and run by the British Council in partnership with IDP Education and Cambridge Assessment English . The IELTS system is highly regarded and widely recognised as the world’s most popular English language proficiency test for higher education and global migration . The test evaluates your proficiency in four areas: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking . There are two main IELTS tests available - IELTS Academic and IELTS General Training . IELTS Academic is designed to measure your English language proficiency and to assess if it is suitable for an academic environment . IELTS General Training is designed to measure your English language proficiency in a practical, everyday context . The right test for you depends on whether your goal is to study or work in your chosen country .
